Welcome to Ald Eva.

## A Family of Entrepreneurs

We are a family of entrepreneurs building a values-led brand around nature, creativity, and practical technology.

Ald Eva exists to show that small family ventures can be both meaningful and disciplined: transparent in process, thoughtful in craft, and focused on long-term impact.

Our work currently grows through three connected pillars:

- **Organics** — transparent beekeeping records, colony health updates, and seasonal decisions shared openly.
- **Craft** — handmade crochet pieces, from playful character makes to practical everyday items.
- **Profiles** — the people behind the work, with clear background, skills, and ongoing learning.

## Our Mission

To build a sustainable family business that combines honest documentation, useful creativity, and continuous improvement.

## What You Can Expect Here

- Frequent updates on colony progress and inspections.
- A growing catalog of crochet creations and experiments.
- Real, human stories of building a family-led entrepreneurial journey.
